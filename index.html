Biography

Mitchell Krock is an assistant professor in the Department of Statistics at the University of Missouri-Columbia. Previously, he was a postdoctoral researcher at Argonne National Laboratory (Mathematics and Computer Science Division, advised by Julie Bessac and Mihai Anitescu) and Rutgers University (Department of Statistics, advised by Michael Stein). He was educated at the University of Colorado-Boulder (PhD in Applied Mathematics, advised by Will Kleiber) and Bradley University (BS in Mathematics, senior thesis advised by Michael Lang).
